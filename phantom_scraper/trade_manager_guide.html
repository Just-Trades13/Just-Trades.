Getting Started

Follow these simple steps to connect your Tradovate account:

Step 1: Log into Tradovate

Go to your Tradovate account and log in with your credentials.

Step 2: Navigate to Application Settings

Look for "Application Settings" in your account menu and click on it.

Step 3: Go to API Access Tab

Click on the "API Access" tab and choose "OAuth Registration".

Step 4: Fill Out the Form

Enter your application details and generate your credentials.

Detailed Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Access Your Tradovate Account

  1. Open your web browser and go to https://tradovate.com
  2. Log in with your Tradovate credentials
  3. Verify you're logged into the correct account (Live or Demo)

Step 2: Find Application Settings

  1. Look for "Application Settings" in your account menu
  2. This is typically found in your profile/settings area
  3. Click on "Application Settings"

Step 3: Navigate to API Access

  1. Click on the "API Access" tab
  2. Select "OAuth Registration"
  3. You'll see a form to register your application

Step 4: Fill Out the OAuth Registration Form

Fill in the following information:

App Title: Trade Manager Redirect URI: http://localhost:8082 Description: (Optional) Trading automation app

Step 5: Generate Your Credentials

  1. Click the "Generate" button
  2. Copy your credentials immediately - they're shown only once!
  3. Save them in a secure location

Step 6: Add to Trade Manager

  1. Go to http://127.0.0.1:8082/accounts
  2. Click "Add Account" or "Edit" existing account
  3. Enter your Tradovate credentials
  4. Enter your Client ID and Client Secret from Step 5
  5. Click "Test Connection"

Common Issues and Solutions

Error: "The app is not registered"

Problem: You're trying to connect without registering an OAuth app first.
Solution: Follow the step-by-step guide to register your OAuth application in Tradovate.

Error: "Connection test failed"

Problem: The credentials you entered don't match your Tradovate account.
Solution: Double-check your username, password, Client ID, and Client Secret.

Can't find "Application Settings"

Problem: You can't locate the Application Settings in your Tradovate account.
Solution: Try looking in these areas:
  • Profile/Settings menu
  • Account Settings
  • API or Developer section

Lost Your Credentials

Problem: You can't see your Client ID and Client Secret anymore.
Solution: Regenerate them in your Tradovate Application Settings. The old credentials will be deactivated.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I need to register an OAuth app?

Tradovate requires you to register an OAuth application before you can use their API. This is a security feature that allows you to control which applications have access to your account.

Is my data safe?

Yes! All credentials are stored securely in your local database. Trade Manager only connects to Tradovate on your behalf, just like you would manually.

Can I use multiple Tradovate accounts?

Yes! You can connect multiple Tradovate accounts (Live and Demo) to Trade Manager.

Do I need separate credentials for Live and Demo?

Yes, each Tradovate account needs its own OAuth registration and credentials.

What happens if I lose my credentials?

You can regenerate them in your Tradovate Application Settings. Just create a new OAuth registration with the same details.
